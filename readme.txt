== Description ==

Bring video to your Wordpress site in minutes.  Baeblemusic provides an entire library of exclusive concerts and session videos, plus thousands of indie and alternative music videos to your site with a simple click.
<p />
The Baeblemusic Plugin extends Baeblemusic's entire video library to your site. You can add the plugin as a page or post to easily and immediately include video on your site.
<p />
<blockquote style = "background: whiteSmoke; border: 1px solid #DADADA; padding: 22px 22px 0; margin-bottom: 22px;">
<h4>The video plugin allows you to select any or all of the video feeds coming from Baeblemusic. These feeds are:</h4><p />
<ul>
<li><b>Trending</b> - Updates every 15 minutes with the most watched videos on the platform</li>
<li><b>Newest</b> - Updates throughout the day as we add videos to the platform</li>
<li><b>Concerts</b> - Exclusive long form video from our featured performances of bands and artists that are on the rise, plus acts you already knew</li>
<li><b>Sessions</b> - Exclusive performances from artists in interesting locations.  Watch them play a few songs and discuss what's on their minds</li>
<li><b>Interviews</b> - Intimate conversations with bands where they talk about their lives on the road, latest albums, and what moves them musically</li>
<li><b>Music Videos</b> - A curated take on some of the most interesting indie / alternative music videos hitting the web</li>
</ul>
</blockquote>

== Installation ==

<ol>
<li> Ensure your WordPress version is up to date</li>
<li> Download baeble-music-plugin.zip file from the plugin home page on WordPress.org</li>
<li> FTP baeble-music-plugin.zip to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory or Upload baeble-music-plugin.zip through the 'Plugins' menu</li>
<li> Unzip baeble-music-plugin.zip inside of the /wp-content/plugins/ directory</li>
<li> Activate the Baeblemusic plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress</li>
<li> Activate the PressTrends plugin through the 'Appearance' menu in WordPress for reporting anonymous aggregated stats (optional)</li>
<li> Configure the Baeblemusic plugin through the 'Settings' menu in WordPress</li>
</ol>

== Frequently asked questions ==

= Where is the Baeblemusic content displayed? =
At the end of a single post page and/or on its own separate page.
= Can I modify the horizontal display width of the thumbnails? =
Yes, as of version 2.0 you can select as few as one or as many as five thumbnails across.
= Can I redistribute the video content? =
You may freely distribute the Baeble plugin, however all content being streamed is copyrighted. 
= How many content categories are available for display? =
Six: Trending, Newest, Concerts, Sessions, Interviews, Music Videos
